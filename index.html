Medicure

The Medicure web application is a tool designed to make healthcare more accessible and convenient for users. It allows people to check their heart condition, track symptoms, and get advice from the right doctor. When users enter their symptoms, the system checks if they match any known diseases and suggests a doctor who can help.
The app also provides live updates on Covid-19, helps users find nearby doctors and hospitals, and suggests specialists based on health issues. By combining these features in one easy-to-use platform, Medicure helps users manage their health and connect with doctors quickly.
